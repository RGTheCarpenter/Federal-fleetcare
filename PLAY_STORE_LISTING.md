# Play Store Listing Copy

## App title

`FleetCare Mobile`

## Short description

`Track company vehicles, maintenance, fuel, drivers, trips, GPS history, and reports.`

## Full description

`FleetCare Mobile helps teams manage company vehicles from one place. Track service records, fuel fills, driver assignments, reminders, GPS checkpoints, trip history, and downloadable reports without switching between spreadsheets and paper notes.

Use FleetCare Mobile to:

- add and manage vehicles and drivers
- record maintenance, invoices, and service photos
- log fuel fills and watch fuel costs
- assign drivers to vehicles
- create due-date and mileage reminders
- capture live GPS checkpoints from the field
- run trip tracking and review saved route history
- download PDF fleet reports for a selected month or custom date range

FleetCare Mobile is built for day-to-day fleet operations and internal business use. It works well for service teams, field crews, and small business fleets that need a simple way to keep vehicle information organized and up to date.`
