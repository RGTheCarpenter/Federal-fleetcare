# Install FleetCare On A Phone

FleetCare is now installable as a phone app through the browser.

## iPhone

1. Open Safari.
2. Go to `https://fleetcare-web.onrender.com`.
3. Tap the Share button.
4. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Tap **Add**.

FleetCare will appear on the home screen like an app.

## Android

1. Open Chrome.
2. Go to `https://fleetcare-web.onrender.com`.
3. Tap the three-dot menu.
4. Tap **Add to Home screen** or **Install app**.
5. Confirm.

FleetCare will appear in the app drawer or on the home screen.

## Notes

- Users still need internet access for live company data.
- Login and company invite code rules still apply.
- If a phone shows an old version, close the browser tab and reopen the app.
